Getting Started & User Guide

What is BMM Cut Test 2.0?

BMM Cut Test tracks the weekly purchase cost of beef cuts and calculates the true cost per pound after trimming. Every cut loses weight during butchering — the yield adjustment gives you the real cost of usable product, not just what you paid at the scale. Version 2.0 adds multi-tenant support, user management, CSV export, and email alerts.

Step 1 — Add Your Cuts

Go to the Cuts tab and click + Add Cut. Enter the cut name (e.g. "Chuck Roll"), an optional category (e.g. "Chuck"), and an optional description. Active cuts appear automatically on the Weekly Update form and the print sheet. To remove a cut, click Delete and enter the password when prompted.

Step 2 — Enter Weekly Prices

Go to Weekly Update, pick the entry date (defaults to today), and fill in three values for each cut:

  • Purchase $/lb — price per pound you paid at purchase
  • Purchase Lbs — total weight purchased (before trimming)
  • Trim Lbs — weight of trim/waste removed during butchering

The app calculates Yield Loss %, Yield %, and True Cost live as you type. Click Save All when done. Saving the same date again updates existing entries. To delete a saved date's entries, click Delete Date and enter the password.

How the Formulas Work

Metric Formula What it means
Yield Loss % Trim Lbs ÷ Purchase Lbs × 100 Percentage of purchased weight lost to trim
Yield % 100 − Yield Loss % Percentage of purchased weight that is usable product
True Cost/lb (Purchase $/lb ÷ Yield %) × 100 Actual cost per usable pound after accounting for trim loss

Example: Chuck Roll at $3.50/lb, 100 lbs purchased, 18 lbs trim → Yield Loss = 18%, Yield = 82%, True Cost = ($3.50 ÷ 82) × 100 = $4.27/lb.

Users Tab

Admins can create and manage users in the Users tab. Each user has a role: Admin (full access) or Butcher (Weekly Update only). Butchers log in with their own username and password. Any user can change their own password via the Password link in the nav bar.

Reports & CSV Export

The Reports tab lets you chart any metric over time. Use Export CSV to download all entry data (filtered by date range) as a spreadsheet.

Password-Protected Actions

Two destructive actions require a password to confirm:

  • Delete Date (Weekly Update) — soft-deletes all entries for a saved date
  • Delete Cut (Cuts tab) — soft-deletes a cut and hides its historical entries
